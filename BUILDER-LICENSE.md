# APIExpose Builder License (per cabinet)

## Purpose

This document defines the per-cabinet builder license for APIExpose. It is the
purchasable instrument of the commercial rules described in
`COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md` for one specific case: **building and selling a
RetroBat-based cabinet or machine to an end customer whose own use remains
personal and non-commercial**.

RetroBat remains free software. This license only concerns APIExpose and its
associated Data Pack (see `DATA-LICENSE.md`).

## Grant

One purchased builder license key grants the builder or reseller the right to:

- preinstall, bundle and configure APIExpose on **one (1)** cabinet, mini-PC,
  drive or preconfigured installation sold to an end customer;
- include the APIExpose Data Pack on that same machine;
- advertise the sold machine as including APIExpose features.

The license key is the proof of the grant. Keep it associated with the sold
machine (invoice reference recommended). One key covers one sold machine;
selling several machines requires one key per machine.

## End customer

The end customer's use of the sold machine falls under the personal and
non-commercial terms of `PERSONAL-LICENSE.md` and `DATA-LICENSE.md`. No
additional license is required from the end customer as long as their use
remains personal.

## What this license does not cover

- Operating cabinets commercially (arcade venue, bar, paid events): this is
  covered by the Fleet Hub venue licenses, not by this document.
- Redistributing APIExpose or the Data Pack outside of a sold machine
  (digital packs, downloads, marketplaces): requires a separate written
  agreement per `COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md`.
- Modifying or rebranding APIExpose or the Data Pack.

## Volume

Volume discounts for builders are handled at purchase time; the per-machine
rule is unchanged. For structured partnerships (white label, preactivation at
scale), contact us for a written agreement.
